Step 3 — Where the money goes
For every ₱100 you pay, here's the split.
📚
Education
Schools, teachers, state universities, DepEd, CHED, TESDA. The biggest slice.
₱895B ₱18 of every ₱100
💸
Debt Payments
Paying back loans the government borrowed. This goes to banks and foreign lenders — not Filipinos.
₱1.28T ₱21 of every ₱100
🏗️
Infrastructure
Roads, bridges, flood control, government buildings, airports. DPWH manages most of this.
₱786B ₱14 of every ₱100
🏥
Health
Public hospitals, PhilHealth, medicines, DOH programs. Underfunded relative to need.
₱310B ₱9 of every ₱100
🤝
Social Services
4Ps cash transfers, DSWD programs, housing, support for poor families.
₱280B ₱8 of every ₱100
🛡️
Defence & Police
AFP, PNP, coast guard, internal security. Salaries and equipment.
₱261B ₱7 of every ₱100

THE GREY AREA
